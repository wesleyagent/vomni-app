## WHAT VOMNI IS

Vomni is a review management platform for service businesses. It automatically sends review requests to customers after every appointment, routes happy customers to Google, and catches unhappy customers privately before they can leave a public negative review.

The product runs on autopilot. The business owner sets it up once in 5 minutes and never touches it again.

---

## THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

Service businesses — barbers, salons, restaurants, dentists, tattoo studios — live and die by their Google rating. But almost none of them have a system for collecting reviews. They rely on customers to do it voluntarily, which almost never happens.

The data:
- 96% of customers read Google reviews before visiting a local business
- 1 bad review drives away an average of 30 potential customers
- Businesses below 4.0 stars lose 70% of search clicks before anyone sees them
- £12,150 in lifetime customer value is lost from a single complaint that goes public
- Only 1 in 25 unhappy customers complains directly — the rest go straight to Google
- Businesses with 25+ reviews earn 108% more revenue than those without
- Every 0.1 star increase in rating = +25% conversion rate
- Crossing 4.0 stars = +20% sales and 2x traffic
- 57% of consumers refuse to use a business rated below 4.0 stars

The result: most service businesses are slowly losing customers to competitors who have more reviews and a higher rating — and they do not even know it is happening.

---

## THE SOLUTION

Vomni fixes this with three steps:

1. The business forwards their booking confirmation emails to their unique Vomni inbox. One email forward. Done forever.
2. 24 hours after every appointment, Vomni automatically sends the customer a personalised SMS asking them to rate their experience.
3. If they rate 4-5 stars, they are redirected to the business's Google review page. If they rate 1-3 stars, their feedback comes privately to the business owner — never reaching Google.

The result: more 5-star Google reviews, zero negative reviews reaching the public, and a dashboard showing everything in real time.

## THE PRODUCT IN DETAIL

**Dashboard overview:**
Shows review requests sent, completion rate, average rating, Google reviews generated, negative feedback caught, and review velocity. Every metric has a plain English explanation and a benchmark comparison.

**Customers section:**
Full list of every booking and review request with status — scheduled, sent, opened, submitted, redirected to Google, or private feedback.

**Feedback inbox:**
All negative feedback (1-3 stars) appears here privately. AI generates a suggested recovery response for each complaint. Business owner can reply, mark in progress, or mark resolved.

**Analytics:**
Charts showing rating distribution, requests over time, completion rate trend, and Google reviews generated. AI insight cards give specific actionable recommendations.

**Settings:**
Business details, Google review link, SMS template, review request timing, notification email, email forwarding address, brand colour.

**Onboarding:**
Five-step checklist guides new customers through setup. Cannot skip to dashboard until first three steps complete.

**"Not interested"**
"No problem at all. Out of curiosity — is it that reviews are not a priority right now, or that you have not found the right way to collect them consistently?"

"Totally fair. One bad review costs the average barber around £12,000 in lost lifetime customer value. Vomni is £70 a month. Most customers see that covered in the first week."

**"I already get reviews"**
"That is great — how many per month? Most businesses get 2-3 naturally. Our customers average 15-20. The difference is just having a system running automatically."

If they push back further and say they still get reviews regularly:
"That is great — but here is the risk you still have. Even if you are getting reviews, every unhappy customer right now is going straight to Google before you even know there is a problem. Vomni does not just collect more reviews — it intercepts unhappy customers privately so you can fix the issue before it becomes a 1-star review. One complaint caught privately is worth more than ten 5-star reviews."

The key insight to always remember: getting reviews is not the same as controlling your reputation. Any business without Vomni is one unhappy customer away from a public complaint they cannot remove. Vomni gives the business owner the chance to fix problems privately before they reach Google.

**"I don't have time to set this up"**
"That is exactly why we built it this way. Setup takes 5 minutes — one email forward. After that you never touch it again."

**"How does it work?"**
This is a buying signal. Respond immediately: "Happy to show you — takes about 15 minutes. When works best for you this week?" Then send the Calendly link.

**"I'll think about it"**
Wait 48 hours then: "Hey [name] — just following up. Happy to answer any questions or show you a quick demo so you can see if it makes sense for [business name]."

**"My customers don't leave reviews"**
"94% of customers will leave a review when asked at the right time and in the right way. The issue is almost never the customers — it is the timing and the ask."

**"I use [competitor]"**
"Good to know. What do you use it for? We find most businesses using other tools are still not getting consistent Google reviews because the SMS timing and gating is not set up right. Happy to show you the difference."

Never offer discounts. Never get defensive. Always redirect toward a demo.
