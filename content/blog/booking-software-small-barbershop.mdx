Most booking software is designed for multi-location salons with staff rotas, complex permissions, and enterprise reporting. If you're a one-chair barber or a two-chair shop, 80% of those features are noise.

## The Non-Negotiables

**Online booking that works from a link** — Clients should be able to book in under 60 seconds from a link you send via WhatsApp or put in your Instagram bio. No account creation required. No app downloads.

**Automated reminders** — A message sent 24–48 hours before each appointment cuts no-shows significantly. This should happen automatically.

**A simple client list you own** — Your booking history and client contact details should be exportable. If you can't get your own data out, you don't own your client relationships.

**Sensible pricing** — A flat monthly fee. Not a percentage of revenue. Not per-booking charges.

## What Small Barbershops Don't Need

- Multi-location management
- Complex staff rota tools
- A marketplace directory listing (once you have Google visibility and a direct booking link, the value is marginal)
- Built-in POS with hardware

The platforms that try to be everything often do nothing brilliantly. For a small shop, simplicity is a feature.

## The Question Worth Asking

Before signing up for any platform, ask: "Can I contact my own clients without going through this platform?"

If the answer is no or costs extra, that's a warning sign. Your client list is your most valuable business asset.

## What to Set Up on Day One

1. Create your booking page with your services and availability
2. Put the booking link in your Instagram bio, WhatsApp Business profile, and Google Business Profile
3. Turn on automated reminders for every appointment
4. Turn on automated review requests post-appointment
5. Test the booking flow yourself from a client perspective

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I really need booking software as a solo barber?**
If you're still managing bookings via WhatsApp manually or taking phone calls, online booking software will save you significant time and reduce no-shows. The ROI is positive from day one for most barbers.

**What's the cheapest booking software for barbers?**
Setmore and Square Appointments have genuinely free tiers. Paid platforms start from around £9–29/month. The right question is cost relative to the value of no-shows prevented and client time saved.

**Will online booking replace the WhatsApp conversation with clients?**
No — and it shouldn't try to. Booking software handles the transactional part. Relationship and communication still happen via WhatsApp.

**How long does it take to set up online booking?**
For a simple solo setup: 30–60 minutes. Services, availability, booking link. Done.
