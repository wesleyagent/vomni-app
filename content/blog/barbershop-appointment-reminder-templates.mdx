The message you send matters less than whether you send it at all — but wording still makes a meaningful difference. Here are the templates that consistently perform well for independent barbershops, with timing guidance for each.

## 1. Booking Confirmation

Send immediately after booking is confirmed.

> Hi [Name], you're booked in with us on [Day] at [Time] for [Service]. See you then! If anything changes, here's your link to reschedule: [link]

## 2. Pre-Appointment Reminder (48 Hours)

> Hey [Name], just a reminder — you're booked with us on [Day] at [Time]. See you then! 💈 Need to reschedule? [link]

The reschedule link is important. It gives the client an easy out — and a rescheduled appointment is better than a no-show.

## 3. Same-Day Reminder (2–4 Hours Before)

> Quick reminder — your appointment's today at [Time]. See you shortly!

Brief. The full confirmation was already sent — this is just a nudge.

## 4. Post-Appointment Review Request (20–30 Minutes After)

> Hey [Name], hope you're happy with the cut! If you've got 30 seconds, a Google review would mean a lot to us 🙏 [direct review link]

The direct review link is non-negotiable. Without it, you lose 70–80% of willing reviewers.

## 5. Rebooking Reminder (Day 28–35)

> Hey [Name] — it's been about a month since your last cut. Ready for a tidy up? Book your slot here: [link] 💈

## 6. Lapsed Client Reactivation (8–10 Weeks)

> Hey [Name], it's been a while — we miss you! If you're due a cut, here's your link to grab a slot: [link]

Don't offer a discount in this first message. The personal acknowledgement alone converts surprisingly well.

## Timing Summary

| Message | When |
|---|---|
| Booking confirmation | Immediately after booking |
| 48-hour reminder | 2 days before appointment |
| Same-day reminder | 2–4 hours before |
| Review request | 20–30 minutes post-appointment |
| Rebooking reminder | Day 28–35 after appointment |
| Lapsed client reactivation | 8–10 weeks after last visit |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Should I use WhatsApp or SMS for reminders?**
WhatsApp where possible — higher open rates, free to send, and clients can reply directly. SMS as a fallback for clients who don't use WhatsApp.

**Can I automate all these messages?**
Yes. Booking software with built-in automation — like Vomni — triggers each message at the right time without any manual action. Set the templates once, and every client gets the right message at the right moment.

**What if a client asks me to stop messaging them?**
Respect the opt-out immediately. Good automation platforms include an unsubscribe mechanism — a client replying STOP should remove them from automated sequences.

**Is there a character limit I need to worry about?**
For WhatsApp: no meaningful limit. For SMS: 160 characters per segment. Keep SMS short.
