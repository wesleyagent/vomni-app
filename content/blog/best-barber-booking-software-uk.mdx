The best barber booking software in the UK depends on one thing: **how you get new clients**. If you rely on marketplace discovery, Fresha or Booksy give you reach but charge a commission. If your clients come from Google, Instagram, and word of mouth, a flat-fee platform keeps more money in your pocket.

## The UK Barber Booking Software Market in 2026

The market has consolidated around a handful of platforms, each with a distinct pricing model:

- **Marketplace-commission platforms:** Fresha, Booksy — free or cheap upfront, charge per new booking
- **Flat-fee platforms:** Vomni, Square Appointments, Acuity — fixed monthly cost, no commission
- **Legacy POS systems:** Salon Iris, Shortcuts — feature-rich, expensive, designed for large multi-location salons

For independent barbershops and small chains, the choice almost always comes down to Fresha, Booksy, or a flat-fee alternative.

## Platform-by-Platform Breakdown

### Fresha
**Best for:** New barbershops with no existing client base who need marketplace discovery

**Pricing:** Free base plan + 20% commission (min £6) on new marketplace clients

**Strengths:**
- Largest UK marketplace
- Slick booking UI
- Good Google Reserve integration
- Client management tools included

**Weaknesses:**
- 20% commission adds up fast
- Clients are shared with Fresha's platform (they market competing shops to your clients)
- Disputes over what counts as a "new" client

**Bottom line:** Excellent for getting started. Expensive once you're established.

---

### Booksy
**Best for:** Barbershops wanting paid promotion and marketplace exposure

**Pricing:** ~£29.99/month + up to 30% commission on Boost bookings

**Strengths:**
- Strong presence in urban UK markets
- Boost can drive significant new-client volume
- Good mobile app for clients

**Weaknesses:**
- Double-cost model (subscription + commission)
- Boost commissions can exceed £400/month at scale
- UI is less polished than Fresha

**Bottom line:** Can be cost-effective in the short term for new businesses. Commission stacks rapidly.

---

### Vomni
**Best for:** Established barbershops wanting to own their client relationships and automate reviews

**Pricing:** From £35/month flat, no commission

**Strengths:**
- Zero per-booking commission
- Automated review collection via WhatsApp/SMS
- Direct booking links (no marketplace middleman)
- Full client data ownership
- Google Reserve integration

**Weaknesses:**
- No marketplace discovery — you drive your own traffic
- Smaller brand recognition than Fresha/Booksy

**Bottom line:** Best economics for shops with 15+ new clients/month from non-marketplace sources.

---

### Square Appointments
**Best for:** Barbershops already using Square for payments

**Pricing:** Free for sole traders, £29/month for teams

**Strengths:**
- Seamless payment integration
- Good for multi-staff scheduling
- No commission

**Weaknesses:**
- No UK marketplace discovery
- Review collection not built-in
- Less barber-specific than alternatives

---

### Acuity Scheduling (Squarespace)
**Best for:** Barbershops with a Squarespace website

**Pricing:** From £16/month

**Strengths:**
- Very flexible booking forms
- Good website embed

**Weaknesses:**
- Not barber-specific — no client history, service tracking
- No marketplace, no review tools

## The Commission vs Subscription Decision

The break-even calculation is simple:

**Monthly new clients × average ticket × 20% = what you'd pay Fresha**

If that number exceeds your target platform's monthly fee, you're better on a subscription. For most active UK barbershops, that break-even is around **12–15 new clients per month**.

## What Actually Drives New Clients for UK Barbershops?

According to independent barber surveys, the top sources of new clients in 2026 are:

1. Google Maps search (the dominant channel for local discovery)
2. Word of mouth
3. Instagram / TikTok
4. Marketplace apps (Fresha, Booksy)

Barbershops with strong Google ratings and active social media rarely need marketplace apps. Their clients find them via search, then book directly.

## Our Recommendation

- **Just starting out, no existing clients:** Start on Fresha's free tier to build your initial client base. Switch platforms once you hit 15+ new clients/month.
- **Established shop, 20+ new clients/month from Google/social:** Migrate to a flat-fee platform. Vomni, Square, or Acuity depending on your review and payment needs.
- **Multi-location chain:** Contact platforms directly — enterprise pricing applies.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best free booking software for barbers in the UK?**
Fresha is the most widely used free booking platform for UK barbers, but 'free' comes with a 20% commission on new clients from its marketplace. For barbers who want genuinely free software without commissions, options are limited — most require either a subscription or accept marketplace fees.

**Which barber booking software has no commission?**
Vomni, Square Appointments, and Acuity Scheduling charge flat monthly fees with no per-booking commissions. Fresha and Booksy both charge marketplace commissions on new clients.

**Can barbers take deposits through booking software?**
Yes — most modern booking platforms (Fresha, Booksy, Vomni, Square) support deposit collection to reduce no-shows. The feature is typically included in paid plans or available as an add-on.

**Does barber booking software integrate with Google Maps?**
Most major platforms support Google Reserve integration, allowing clients to book directly from your Google Business Profile. This is available on Fresha, Booksy, and Vomni.

**How do I move my client list from Fresha to another platform?**
Fresha allows you to export your client list as a CSV from the client management section. Most alternative platforms (including Vomni) accept CSV imports and can migrate your data during onboarding.
